BUBBLES

BUBBLES is a chimpanzee once kept as a pet by the American singer Micheal Jackson who bought him from a Texas research facility in the 1980s.

ABOUT

BUBBLES is a community-driven meme coin built for fun, virality, and purpose, based on the legendary BUBBLES the chimp.

BUBBLES was initially kept at the Jackson familyhome in Encisco, LA but was moved to the Neverland Ranch in 1988.

In 2003, Bubbles was moved to the Center for Great Apes in Wauchula, Florida, where he lives to this day, or so we thought...

BUBBLES has mysteriously gone missing, there was no forced entry, no disturbed habitat, The only clue left behind was a perfectly scorched circle in the ground, and a note that read "I am coming for you".

We are on a mission to find BUBBLES, and we need your help! Join us in our quest to uncover the truth behind BUBBLES' disappearance.
